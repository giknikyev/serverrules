Server Rules
1. No offensive language or behavior.
Avoid using any offensive, hateful, or discriminatory language. Treat others with respect at all times.
2. Respect all players and staff.
Ensure that you are polite and considerate towards all members of the community, including staff.
3. No cheating, exploiting, or using hacks.
Cheating and exploiting game mechanics is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
4. Follow the roleplay guidelines at all times.
Adhere to the server's roleplay rules to ensure an immersive experience for everyone.
5. No spamming or advertising.
Avoid spamming the chat and refrain from posting unauthorized advertisements.
6. Respect the decisions of administrators.
Admin decisions are final. If you have concerns, raise them respectfully.
7. Avoid disrupting the server's atmosphere.
Ensure that your actions do not negatively impact the enjoyment of others on the server.
